Manali

Manali, a picturesque hill station in Himachal Pradesh, is famous for its stunning landscapes and adventure activities. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains and lush valleys, it offers skiing and paragliding in Solang Valley, serene treks to Bhrigu Lake, and the charming Hadimba Temple. Visitors can also relax in the natural hot springs at Vashisht and explore the quaint streets of Old Manali filled with cafes and shops.
