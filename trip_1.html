Mussoorie

Mussoorie is a beautiful hill station located in the northern Indian state of Uttarakhand. Nestled in the Himalayas, it offers stunning views, pleasant weather, and a charming colonial ambiance. Popular attractions include Camel's Back Road, Kempty Falls, and Gun Hill. It's a great spot for trekking, sightseeing, and enjoying the lush greenery.
